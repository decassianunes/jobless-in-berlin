Jobless in Berlin

Beta

v1.0 · still growing

My role got impacted. After 5 years, I had nowhere to go, no task to do, no meeting to attend.

I'm an immigrant. My social life was mostly built around work. When the job disappeared, a lot of the structure around my day disappeared with it — and the city suddenly felt too big.

So I built this. Not to find a place — but to figure out how to live this new life.

💡 How it works

Filter by how you're feeling. Tap a vibe — quiet, free, focus, sun — and the map shows you what fits.

Know a good spot?

This is not a finished app and no place paid to be here. If you know somewhere that should be on the map, send it over.
