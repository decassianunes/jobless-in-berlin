# Jobless in Berlin

A responsive, interactive map for people figuring out where to go — and who they
are — after losing a job. Filter cafés, parks, libraries, free museums, job
centers, mensas, gyms, and pools/lakes in Berlin by how you feel and what you
need from the day.

Built with HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript, and the Google Maps API. No framework,
no build step, no sponsorships — just a real problem and a reason to learn.
